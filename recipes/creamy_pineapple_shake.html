Creamy Pineapple Shake

Description

This delicious shake is the perfect blend of tropical delight and summer flavors. If you need something to cool off while in the hot sun be sure to add this drink to your list.

Ingredients

  • 1 (20 ounce) can DOLEÂ® Crushed Pineapple, undrained
  • 1 pint vanilla ice cream or frozen yogurt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 dash Dash ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Combine crushed pineapple, ice cream, milk and cinnamon in blender or food processor container. Cover; blend until smooth.
